Born in Itumbiara-Goiás - Brazil, I am a Freelance Front-end Developer, currently dedicated to my training in Software Engineering at the UNINTER University of São Paulo.

My professional career covers a wide range of experiences, including commercial areas, sales, prospecting, strategies and after-sales, with a focus on excellence in the customer experience and ensuring the quality of products and services. Furthermore, I have technical training from Senai as a Logistics Technician, which broadens my multidisciplinary vision.

Since my teens, I have maintained an ongoing interest in technology, which led me to specialize in web programming, networking and IT infrastructure, as well as software design. I recognize that the search for knowledge is a constant and essential path to professional improvement.

One of my main objectives is to share my knowledge and experience, especially teaching programming to young people and children, thus aiming to contribute significantly to the educational and technological development of society.

I constantly seek to improve myself, applying the latest techniques and technologies in my daily work. Soon, I plan to share my journey and learnings through platforms like YouTube.

If you believe that I can add value to your project or company, I am available for a productive conversation. I value the opportunity to exchange experiences and collaborate on projects that promote mutual growth and innovation.
